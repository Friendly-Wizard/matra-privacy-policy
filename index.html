Privacy Policy for Matra

Effective date: July 4, 2026

Overview

Matra is a local-only sound level estimation app for Android. It is designed to estimate sound level, track exposure, and save measurement sessions on your device.

Microphone

Matra uses the phone microphone only to estimate sound level in real time. Matra does not record, store, or transmit voice or raw audio.

Saved data

Matra can save measurement sessions on the device. Saved sessions contain measurement summaries and sound level trace data only. They do not contain audio.

Data sharing

Matra does not use an account, cloud sync, analytics, ads, or third-party tracking. Matra does not upload, sell, or share user data.

Data storage and deletion

Saved sessions and settings stay on your device. You can delete saved sessions inside the app. You can also remove locally stored app data by uninstalling Matra or clearing the app data through Android system settings.

Security

Matra does not transmit user data to a server. Data created by the app remains in local app storage on your device.

Measurement limitations

Matra provides phone-based sound level estimates. It is not a certified sound level meter or certified dosimeter. Exposure estimates are not for workplace compliance, legal, medical, or safety decisions. Calibration can improve consistency for a specific phone, but it does not certify the phone.

Children

Matra is not directed to children.
